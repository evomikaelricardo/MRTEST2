Advanced Analytics Features

Comprehensive retail intelligence powered by cutting-edge technology

Footfall Traffic

Monitor customer footfall patterns with real-time traffic counting and visitor analytics. Track incoming visitors, hourly trends, and visitor flow patterns to optimize store operations.

Movement Tracking

Advanced movement tracking provides insights into customer journey paths throughout your retail space. Understand how customers navigate your store and optimize product placement for maximum exposure.

Heatmap Analysis

Visualize customer activity with detailed heatmap analysis showing the most and least visited areas. Use color-coded visualizations to identify hot zones and make data-driven store layout decisions.

Predictive Analysis

Leverage machine learning algorithms to predict future customer behavior and traffic patterns. Anticipate busy periods and proactively optimize staffing, inventory, and marketing campaigns.

Demographic Analysis

Analyze customer demographics including age groups, gender distribution, and customer profiles. Understand your customer base composition to tailor marketing strategies and product offerings for different demographic segments.

Cashier Analysis

Monitor cashier performance and checkout efficiency with detailed analytics on transaction times, queue lengths, and service quality. Optimize checkout processes and staff training to reduce wait times and improve customer satisfaction.

Store Front Conversion

Track window shopping conversion rates and analyze how effective your storefront displays are at attracting customers inside. Measure the impact of window displays, signage, and storefront marketing on foot traffic conversion.
